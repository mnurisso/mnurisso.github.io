About me

Welcome to my homepage!
I'm Matteo Nurisso, a Ph.D. candidate at International School for Advanced Studies (SISSA).
I mainly work on Computational Astrophysics, with the primary goal of investigating high-energy emissions from extragalactic sources through (relativistic) magneto-hydrodynamical simulations (MHD).

Scientific interests

My doctoral research focuses on the implementation of the acceleration of non-thermal component of particles due to magnetic reconnection in magnetohydrodynamical (MHD) simulations, with the use of macro-particles to describe the behavior of the non-thermal component evolving concurrently with the fluid. I work with the MHD code PLUTO.

Activism and Hobbies

I've always felt the need to be active part of the society, with my work and during my free time.
I started being a member of the Scout association since 2000. From 2013 to 2015 I have been Scoutmaster, being responsible for the activities and educations of kids in the primary school.
Since 2013 I'm mostly involved in Silviadizenzero, an association whose primary goal are Climate Science popularization and spreading of Vegetarian Culture. Our main activities are the organization of workshops, meetings with experts, movie projections and collaborations with other associations in Italy. The main goal is to spread scientific culture about climate change and what can be done by individuals both with everyday decisions and lobbying.
Since 2021 we collaborate with a regional radio, Radio Dora, creating and presenting a weekly live content on Climate change awareness.
Since 2018 I'm Vice-president of this association.
